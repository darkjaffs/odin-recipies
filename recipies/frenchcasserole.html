French Toast Casserole

Description

French Toast Casserole offers a delightful twist on the classic breakfast favorite. Slices of bread are soaked in a luscious mixture of eggs, milk, vanilla, and cinnamon, allowing the flavors to meld and infuse into every bite. The soaked bread is then layered in a baking dish and often topped with a sweet and buttery streusel or a drizzle of maple syrup. This casserole is perfect for a leisurely brunch or a special occasion breakfast, as it combines the comforting taste of traditional French toast with the convenience of a casserole, making it easy to serve a crowd. The result is a warm, custardy, and cinnamon-infused treat that captures the essence of a cozy morning meal.

Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar
  • Butter
  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla
  • Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Cook the butter with a cup of brown sugar until the butter is melted and the sugar is dissolved, then pour the mixture into a prepared baking dish. Scatter the bread pieces over the butter mixture. Mix the milk, vanilla, and eggs together, then pour over the bread.
  2. Cover the casserole and transfer to the fridge. Refrigerate at least 8 hours or up to overnight.
  3. Remove the casserole from the fridge and uncover. Sprinkle with a mixture of brown sugar-cinnamon, then bake in the preheated oven until the casserole is browned and bubbling.
