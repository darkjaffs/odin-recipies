Green Bean Casserole

Description

Green bean casserole is a classic and beloved American dish, especially popular during holiday gatherings. The dish typically consists of fresh green beans that are blanched to retain their crispness, combined with a rich and creamy mushroom sauce made with condensed cream of mushroom soup. The casserole is often topped with crispy fried onions, adding a delightful crunch and savory flavor. The combination of tender green beans, flavorful mushroom sauce, and crispy onions creates a comforting and iconic side dish that has become a staple on many festive tables.

Ingredients

  • Canned Green Beans
  • Condensed Mushroom Soup
  • French-fried Onions
  • Shredded Cheddar Cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Mix the beans and soup in a microwave-safe bowl and microwave until warm.
  3. Stir in half the cheese. Microwave until melted and well-blended.
  4. Transfer to a prepared baking dish. Top with fried onions and remaining cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until the cheese is melted and the onions are brown.
